The Venerable Jabula Palace - Manzawayo Area

Discover the Beauty of Manzawayo Area

Welcome to the land of the Great Manqele tribe, the generation of the great man Jubisa. Manzawayo is a village with warm-hearted people, a place rich in history and forestry, perfect for nature lovers.

Explore the vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and fascinating history that make Manzawayo Area a must-visit destination. Whether you enjoy hiking, swimming, or simply soaking in the beauty of nature, Manzawayo has something for everyone.

Come and witness the majestic beauty of our first waterfall, a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Our second waterfall offers a serene environment and a refreshing experience for those who enjoy a good swim in nature.

For the adventurers, our mountains provide hiking trails with breathtaking views.

Rivers

Our pristine rivers are perfect for swimming and relaxing, surrounded by stunning natural beauty.
